| Assignment | Title | Methods |

---

##  Project Summaries

### Team Assignment 1 - Influencer Marketing Performance
Analysed 600 sponsored social media posts from a digital marketing 
campaign across Instagram and TikTok. Built separate regression models 
to explain engagement rate, click-through probability, and comment count. 
Key finding: TikTok, short video format, and CTAs were the strongest 
independent drivers of post performance.

### Team Assignment 2 - Brand Social Media Content Analysis *(in progress)*
Analysing 600 posts from three consumer brands using NLP tools to extract 
sentiment, topic classification, and emoji usage — then modelling how 
these drive likes and comments across and within brands.
